About Us

We Are DMN Solutions Kenya

Founded in 2024, DMN Solutions has been at the forefront of providing innovative IT solutions to businesses across Kenya and beyond. Our team of experienced developers, designers, and digital marketers work together to deliver exceptional results.

We believe in the power of technology to transform businesses and create new opportunities. Our mission is to help our clients leverage technology to achieve their goals and stay ahead of the competition.

  • M-Pesa, eCitizen & KRA integration specialists
  • Full-stack SaaS platform development
  • Responsive, mobile-first design approach
  • Ongoing support & maintenance packages

Our Policies

Privacy Policy

At DMN Solutions Kenya, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you request a quote, contact us, or use our services.

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Business name and project requirements
  • Payment information (processed securely via M-Pesa)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, process transactions, and communicate with you about your projects.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Terms of Service

By engaging our services, you agree to these terms. Please read them carefully.

Service Agreement

Each project will be governed by a separate service agreement outlining scope, timelines, and payment terms.

Payment Terms

  • 50% upfront deposit required to begin work
  • Remaining balance due upon project completion
  • Payments accepted via M-Pesa and bank transfer

Intellectual Property

Upon full payment, you own all rights to the final deliverables. DMN Solutions retains the right to showcase the work in our portfolio.

Revisions

Each project includes up to 3 rounds of revisions within the agreed scope. Additional revisions may incur extra charges.

Refund Policy

We strive for complete client satisfaction. Our refund policy is designed to be fair to both parties.

Eligibility

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of project delivery
  • Upfront deposits are non-refundable once work has commenced
  • Full refunds available if work has not yet started

Process

To request a refund, contact us via WhatsApp or email with your project details. We will review and respond within 48 hours.

Disputes

Any disputes will first be handled through direct negotiation. We are committed to finding a fair resolution for all parties.
